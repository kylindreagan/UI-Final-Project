ABOUT US:

        
Welcome to Maryville College's programming team! Founded in 2002, our team has established itself as a dynamic force in the world of 
            programming contests across the nation. With a passion for problem-solving and a dedication to excellence, we continuously strive to push the
            boundaries of our abilities and the coding world. Through weekly rigorous training, collaborative efforts, and staying up-to-date on all 
            things tech, we equip our members with the skills necessary to succeed in the ever-evolving field of programming. 
            Join the revolution of code and help us nurture talent, foster innovation, and make a name for ourselves in the competitive world of programming.
